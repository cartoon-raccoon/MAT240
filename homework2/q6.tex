\textbf{(Q6)}

\begin{proof}
    We formulate a homogenous system from the set given to us:

    \[
        \begin{cases}
            a\mathbf{x} + c\mathbf{y} = 0\\
            b\mathbf{x} + d\mathbf{y} = 0
        \end{cases}
    \]

    We can then row-reduce:

    \[
        \begin{pmatrix}
            a & c & \vert & 0\\
            b & d & \vert & 0
        \end{pmatrix}
        \rightarrow
        \begin{pmatrix}
            1 & \frac{c}{a} & \vert & 0\\
            b & d & \vert & 0
        \end{pmatrix}
        \rightarrow
        \begin{pmatrix}
            1 & \frac{c}{a} & \vert & 0\\
            0 & \frac{ad - bc}{a} & \vert & 0
        \end{pmatrix}
    \]

    Since $ad - bc \neq 0$, this is not yet in RREF and we continue
    to row-reduce.

    \[
        \begin{pmatrix}
            1 & \frac{c}{a} & \vert & 0\\
            0 & 1 & \vert & 0
        \end{pmatrix}
        \rightarrow
        \begin{pmatrix}
            1 & 0 & \vert & 0\\
            0 & 1 & \vert & 0
        \end{pmatrix}
    \]

    Which forms a linearly independent set.
\end{proof}
