\textbf{(Q9)}

\textbf{False}. Consider the matrices
\[
    \begin{pmatrix}
        2 & 5 & 1\\
        0 & 3 & 4\\
        0 & 0 & 1
    \end{pmatrix}\quad\quad
    \begin{pmatrix}
        2 & 4 & 2\\
        0 & 3 & 3\\
        0 & 0 & 1
    \end{pmatrix}
\]

From these two matrices we obtain:
\[
    AB = \begin{pmatrix}
        4 & 23 & 20\\
        0 & 9  & 13\\
        0 & 0  & 1 
    \end{pmatrix}\quad
    BA = \begin{pmatrix}
        4 & 22 & 20\\
        0 & 9  & 15\\
        0 & 0  & 1 
    \end{pmatrix}
\]
$AB$ and $BA$ have the same eigenvalues but are clearly not equal.
